Why it matters

Small. Daily. Eternal.

Torah learned in someone's merit is one of the most powerful forms of zechus. Here is why this works.

01

Consistency is the key

A single Mishnah a day is 365 Mishnayos a year. Small, daily learning compounds into something extraordinary — and eternal.

02

Torah as a tikkun

The word "Mishna" shares its letters with "Neshama." The Aruch HaShulchan writes that this learning is a great tikkun for the soul.

03

Personal & dedicated

This is not automated software. Every session is learned with your name — or that of your loved one — specifically in mind. Real kavana, every day.

04

Direct access

Questions, special requests, or updates — reach me directly on WhatsApp or email. Personal service, always.

The merit of Torah

Have Torah learned in your merit

Torah learned in the zechus of someone opens heavenly channels that nothing else can. Choose your intention:

🕯️

L'ilui Nishmas

An eternal zechus for a departed soul. Mishnayos are among the greatest gifts you can give a neshamah in the next world.

💊

Refuah Shleimah

Torah learned in someone's merit is among the most powerful sources of heavenly protection, healing, and renewed strength.

💍

Shidduch

Daily learning dedicated to finding one's zivug is a beloved and proven segulah passed down through the generations.

🌿

Parnossah & Hatzlocha

Torah is the source of all blessing. Learning in your merit opens doors of success and livelihood in ways nothing else can.

👶

Zera Shel Kayama

For those davening for children — Torah learning is a powerful zechus and tefillah combined into one daily act of dedication.

What the sources say

Rooted in Torah tradition

"
ונהגו ללמוד משניות הן כל שנים עשר חודש, הן ביום היארצייט, אחר נשמתם. והוא תיקון גדול לנשמה, כי "משנה" אותיות "נשמה"
Aruch HaShulchan — Y"D 376:13
"
Mishnayos help to achieve a kaparah — both for the one learning them, and for the one that they are being learned for.
Chidah & Rav Tzadok HaKohen
"
Make your Torah study fixed, and say little but do much.
Pirkei Avot — 1:15
